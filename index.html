ABOUT WONDERSENSE GAMES

WHO WE ARE

Hi, my name is Tanner Kopel, and I am founder and CEO of WonderSense Games, a one-man virtual reality game studio based in Dallas, Texas. I started this game studio in order to build high-quality VR experiences that truly immerse the player in lively, dynamic worlds. WonderSense Games is named after the metaphor "sense of wonder", which is a feeling of awe and amazement that we hope to recreate in all of our games.

I began programming when I was just 15 years old during the COVID-19 pandemic, and specialized in front end web development for almost 2 years. However, when I watched Mark Zuckerberg's vision for the future of the metaverse at Facebook's connect 2021, I fell in love with the idea of virtual reality. I immediately began working on my first game, The Heist VR, with no experience in game development, and eventually started my own business to keep building even more immersive VR experiences.

SENSE OF WONDER noun

A feeling of awakening or awe triggered by an expansion of one's awareness of what is possible or by confrontation with the vastness of space and time. This is the feeling that inspired me to start this game studio, and this is the feeling that I hope to capture in my future games.

OUR GAMES

01

FAST-PACED ARCADE SHOOTER

THE HEIST VR

The Heist VR is a fun, fast-paced arcade shooter for the Oculus Quest. Play as an experienced thief as you infiltrate 3 different locations and escape with the cash. You must defend yourself from the incoming police as you wait for your bomb to explode and force your way through the armored doors.

02

ACTION-BASED ARCADE DRIVER

DARE TO DRIVE

Enjoy advanced driving mechanics and immersive combat physics as you drive and fight off enemies throughout the world. You must play through the levels, completing various missions such as delivering merchandise, competing in races, or assassinating villanous figures.
